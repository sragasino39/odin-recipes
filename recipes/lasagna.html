Lasagna Recipe

This is one of my most favorite Italian dishes!

This lasagna recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozerella cheese.

This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well!

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat Sauce
  • Lasagna

Steps

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish.
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles).
  3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top.
  4. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
  5. Repeat the same layers two more times.
  6. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover and bake at 375Â° for 30 minutes.
  8. Remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes. Broil at the end if desired.
  9. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
